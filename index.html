About Me

My name is Will Sims. I am an apprentice software developer who enjoys designing and buidling software that solves real problems. I enjoy the collaborative process of thinking about how a system should be built and designed just as much as the process of actually writing the software and building the system. I've always had an interest in software, but I never considered it as a candidate for a career until I began studying Information Management. During my last year and a half at UT, I took classes that involved building databases for a real company. That was the time when I began pursuing a career in software development. Now that I've been exposed to and have completed a full-stack bootcamp, I've been able to integrate many different, smaller pieces of knowledge about software into a bigger, more complete picture.

MyGrades is an application for students to keeps track of the grades he or she receive in their college or highschool courses and calculates their GPA. The "What Do I Need" feature allows a student to calculate a grade that they need to get on an upcoming assignment (homework, exam, project, etc.) in order to get a specific grade (that the user inputs) in the course.
